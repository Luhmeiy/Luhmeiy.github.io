About Me

"Hello there." - Obi-Wan Kenobi

My name is Luiz Henrique Medeiros and I'm a web developer, web designer and a very lazy writer. And yes, I'm a Star Wars fan.

I'm constantly on the looks for a new and exciting technology to test out. I also love to work on small, but interesting, projects.

Stuff that I Love

Coding

Coding has been my passion since I discovered it around 2017. Since then I have tried a little bit of everything and now I can proudly say that I'm able to write codes in HTML, CSS, JS and Python and develop with frameworks such as Bootstrap and JQuery.

Writing

My journey with writing started really soon, when I was about 8 years old. Back then I wrote short stories and tried drawing bad scripted comics. Now, more than ten years later, I'm constantly fighting myself to work on only a project at a time.

In 2020, I published my first book "O Brilho", a book about a boy who finds the diary of a missing teenager that could, in thesis, prove that the town's most famous myth is actually real.

Silly Things

To be honest, who doesn't? I love to work on mini projects just to make my friends laugh, ranging from a code made only to infuriate the user to a MCU style universe composed of super heroes with stupid powers.
